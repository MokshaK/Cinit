# Project Wiki — Lessons Log

> Append-only record of what we learned the hard way during this project.
> Newest entries at the top.
> Format and ritual: see §6 of [`PROJECT_WIKI_SCHEMA.md`](../../PROJECT_WIKI_SCHEMA.md).

## When to append an entry

Any of:
- An incident cost >30 minutes.
- The user gave explicit corrective or surprisingly positive feedback.
- A commit had to be undone, reverted, or hot-fixed within a session.
- The agent caught and corrected its own mistake mid-task, and the mistake would plausibly recur.
- A user-perceived bug turned out to be runtime-state or premise issue — that's a lesson about diagnosis.

Capture is **cheap and loose during the project.** Filtering happens at end-of-project review (`/lessons-review`), not at capture time.
